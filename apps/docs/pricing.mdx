## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my trace quota?">
Soft cap at 100% (you get an alert). Overages bill at $1.20 per 10K traces up to 5× your included quota. At 5× we return HTTP 429 + a Slack alert. No surprise bills.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why are seats capped on Team and Business?">
Honest pricing. We bundle 10 seats on Team (additional seats $19/mo each, contact sales above 25) and 25 on Business (additional $19/mo, contact sales above 50). Enterprise is unlimited.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What inline guardrails run on every request?">
A layer of heuristic guardrails runs inline at the gateway on every request — MCP rug-pull detection, lethal-trifecta taint tracking, prompt-injection patterns, browser stuck-loop, A2A/A2UI validation, argument drift, and tool-schema checks — each can flag or halt the request at the gateway, inline, per the configured policy. A multi-model ML ensemble (Llama Prompt Guard 2 ONNX + a fine-tuned classifier) is on the roadmap to augment the heuristic layer.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does Tracelane compare to LangSmith Engine (launched May 13, 2026)?">
Engine clusters production failures after they happen, then drafts PRs and online evaluators in your LangSmith UI. Tracelane sits one step earlier in the agent lifecycle: inline heuristic guardrails at the gateway (multi-model ML ensemble on the roadmap), tamper-evident promotion record on every deploy, and auto-rollback on objective metrics (cost, latency, error rate, guardrail-fire rate). Engine requires the LangSmith trace surface + LangChain-managed inference keys per LangSmith AI ToS. Tracelane is OTel GenAI semconv-native, BYOK zero markup, Apache 2.0 full stack, self-hostable. See [vs LangSmith Engine](/comparisons/vs-langsmith-engine).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When are PR7–PR12 available?">
PR7–PR10 (trajectory embedding, arg drift, A2A handoff, inline 1B distilled judge) and PR11–PR12 (SLO drift sentinel, LangGraph predictive branch) are on the post-launch roadmap. Free, Builder, Team, and Business get them upon ship without re-contracting.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the Audit SKU and do I need it?">
Tamper-evident agent ledger for regulated AI deployments under EU AI Act Article 12 record-keeping obligations for high-risk AI systems (the Annex III high-risk timeline is provisional — expected around December 2027; confirm against the current text of the Act). Every span's Merkle root is recorded in a tamper-evident hash chain with Ed25519 per-tenant signing today; anchored batches carry a resolved Sigstore Rekor v2 inclusion proof on a best-effort basis, with universal per-run anchoring and daily anchoring to an eIDAS Qualified Trust Service Provider (GlobalSign / ADACOM / Evidency) on the roadmap. If you deploy high-risk AI in the EU, you need this. See [Audit Ledger](/audit-ledger).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial for paid tiers?">
Free hosted (10K traces/mo) is permanently free for non-commercial use. Qualified-free (full Builder tier) for verified OSS projects, .edu emails, and startups with <$1M ARR. No paid-tier free trial — the free hosted tier and OSS self-host are the trial.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I self-host?">
Yes. Apache 2.0 full stack on GitHub (`github.com/tracelane/tracelane`). Self-host the full stack with a single-node Docker Compose (ClickHouse-backed) — no managed cloud required. Enterprise gets a commercial-support license. See [Self-hosting](/self-hosting).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why is HIPAA an add-on?">
Hetzner does not sign Business Associate Agreements. Our HIPAA-eligible deployment runs on GCP us-central1 (BAA signed) at $2,000/mo on top of Enterprise base.
