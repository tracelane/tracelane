# Code of Conduct

Tracelane adopts the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

The full text is available at:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be sent to security@tracelane.dev.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

All community members are expected to comply with the Contributor Covenant.
